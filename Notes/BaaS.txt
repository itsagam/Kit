Solutions
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
	- Native:
		- Ultimate Mobile Pro: https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/tools/integration/ultimate-mobile-pro-130345
		- Cross Platform Native Plugins: https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/tools/integration/cross-platform-native-plugins-ultra-pack-31086
		- Easy Mobile Pro: https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/tools/integration/easy-mobile-pro-75476

	- Unity: Free, but multiplayer costs 0.49$/gb bandwidth above 20 concurrent players (https://unity3d.com/unity/features/multiplayer)
		Pros: Supported by Unity, Free
		Cons: Disjointed and raw

	- App42: Free at reasonable limits (http://api.shephertz.com/pricing.php)
		Pros: Lots of high-level features
		Cons: SDK not updated for over half an year

	- PlayFab: Free for Essential features (https://playfab.com/pricing/)
		Pros: No limits on services offered in Free tier
		Cons: API is limited, depends on third-party integrations like Photon

	- GameSparks: Free for just 10 concurrent players, 10 requests/s & 10 MB storage (https://www.gamesparks.com/pricing/)
		Pros: Server-side coding, multiplayer support, quite stable
		Cons: High pricing with no Free tier

	- Herioc Labs/Nakama: Free and open-source (https://heroiclabs.com/managed-cloud/)
		Pros: Server scripting with Lua, clean code & async calls, multiplayer
		Cons: Needs to be self-hosted

	- Firebase: Free at reasonable limits (https://firebase.google.com/pricing/)
		Pros: Supported by Google, server-side coding, a lot of cloud functions
		Cons: Non-cloud functions are limited

	- BrainCloud: Free at 100 DAU, 2m requests, 200mb storage & 2gb transfer (http://getbraincloud.com/pricing-overview/)
		Pros: High-level features are adequate, updated frequently
		Cons: Not that well-known: long-term support unknown
